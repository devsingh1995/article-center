Drink Water

Drinking water can keep you alert and fired up all day long. Many people aim to drink eight to 10 glasses of water per day, but the amount each person needs can vary depending on the level of physical activity and the climate. You can keep your body hydrated by replacing your usual coffee or soda with a glass of water. You can stay full and resist the temptation to snack by drinking more water at work.

Workout

Staying active and working out every day boosts your circulation throughout your body. The increased blood flow makes you feel more energetic, fresh, and mentally active. It is important to exercise to keep your body fit and healthy. A simple walk with your pet or a daily morning walk is enough. The most important thing is to make it a daily habit. 

Sound Sleep

It is essential to get enough sleep to maintain optimal productivity and overall health. Adequate sleep is important for improving memory, concentration, and decision-making abilities. Many busy entrepreneurs sacrifice sleep in order to meet the demands of their work. In the long run, prioritising sleep can actually help boost productivity.
